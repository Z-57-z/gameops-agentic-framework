# Compensation Policy

## Standard compensation limits

Support may issue low-value consumables for verified minor inconvenience. Premium currency compensation above 500 gems requires ops lead approval. Account-wide or server-wide compensation requires incident commander approval and a written reason.

## Incident compensation

For login or payment incidents under 15 minutes, publish an acknowledgement only unless measurable player loss is confirmed. For incidents from 15 to 60 minutes, prepare a compensation proposal and route it to the ops lead. For incidents above 60 minutes or involving payment loss, escalate to the incident commander.

## Player communication

Do not promise premium currency, refunds, bans, rollbacks, or account changes before approval. Use conditional language: "we are checking eligibility" and "we will follow the published compensation policy".
