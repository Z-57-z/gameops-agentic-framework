# Event Rebate Policy

## Missed recharge rebate rewards

Support may verify event eligibility, recharge timestamp, server id, account id, and event id. Support must not promise a replacement reward until eligibility is confirmed in logs. If eligibility is confirmed and the reward delivery job failed, support may promise manual delivery within one business day.

## Eligibility rules

Recharge rebate rewards require the player to recharge during the published event window and claim on the same server where the recharge occurred. Cross-server claims are not eligible unless the event configuration explicitly says otherwise.

## Exception handling

Exceptions require ops lead approval when the player missed the claim because of a service incident, incorrect announcement copy, or confirmed event configuration error. Unsupported goodwill promises must be marked for human review.
