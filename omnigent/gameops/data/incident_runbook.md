# Incident Runbook

## Severity levels

SEV1 means payment, login, or core gameplay is unavailable for many players. SEV2 means a major feature or event is degraded. SEV3 means a limited issue with a workaround. Login incidents lasting 30 minutes should be treated at least as SEV2.

## Communication cadence

Post the first internal update within 10 minutes of confirmation. For SEV1, update every 15 minutes. For SEV2, update every 30 minutes. Public announcements require incident commander approval when they mention compensation, rollback, or payment impact.

## Escalation path

Assign an incident commander, owner for game server investigation, owner for player support messaging, and owner for compensation proposal when player loss is likely.
