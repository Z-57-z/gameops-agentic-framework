# Campaign Checklist

## Launch readiness

Before launching a weekend event, confirm start time, end time, target servers, eligibility rules, reward table, localization, announcement copy, support FAQ, rollback plan, and monitoring dashboard. Do not launch if reward rules differ between configuration and announcement copy.

## Announcement review

Public announcement copy must include event window, eligibility, reward timing, claim method, limitations, and support channel. Compensation language must not be included unless approved by ops lead.

## Rollback plan

Every campaign launch needs an owner, rollback trigger, rollback steps, and player communication draft. High-value reward events require a dry-run on staging before production launch.
